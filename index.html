Welcome, dear guests

We are so grateful you have travelled near and far to celebrate this day with us. Below you will find everything you need to know about the day's programme. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to reach out to our wedding coordinator, Sarah, at sarah@example.com.

The Day

All times are approximate — please arrive a few minutes early for each event.

  1. 12:00 pm

    Guests Arrive & Welcome Drinks

    The Rose Garden, Rosewood Manor

    Settle in with a glass of Pimm's or elderflower cordial and enjoy the gardens while waiting for the ceremony to begin.

  2. 1:00 pm

    Wedding Ceremony

    The Grand Chapel, Rosewood Manor

    Please be seated by 12:50 pm. The ceremony will last approximately 30 minutes. Photography is welcome after the ceremony — please follow the photographer's guidance during.

  3. 1:30 pm

    Drinks Reception & Canapés

    The Terrace

    Celebrate with champagne, canapés, and lawn games while the wedding party takes photographs in the grounds.

  4. 3:30 pm

    Wedding Breakfast

    The Orangery

    A three-course seated meal, accompanied by speeches from the best man, father of the bride, and the groom. Dietary requirements have been noted from your RSVP — please speak with a member of staff if you have any concerns.

  5. 5:30 pm

    Speeches & Cake Cutting

    The Orangery

    Raise a glass for the toasts and gather round for the cutting of a four-tier lemon and elderflower cake.

  6. 6:30 pm

    Evening Reception Opens

    The Ballroom

    Evening guests are welcomed. The ballroom will be open for dancing, with the bar serving until midnight.

  7. 7:00 pm

    First Dance & Band

    The Ballroom

    Join us on the dancefloor after the first dance. The live band will play until 10:30 pm, followed by a DJ until midnight.

  8. 9:00 pm

    Evening Buffet

    The Terrace Room

    A late-night spread of street food-style favourites, including a fish & chip station and a dessert table.

  9. 12:00 am

    Last Dance & Carriages

    The Ballroom

    The evening draws to a close. Taxis can be pre-booked through the venue — please see a member of staff for details. We hope you have a wonderful evening!
